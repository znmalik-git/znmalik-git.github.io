Openlink Energy Trading including Derivatives

About this case study

Support growth & reduce time to market in Energy trading across Gas, LNG, Power & Emission

Complete transformation of trading landscape from front to back that consolidates multiple systems into single platform and leverages near real time integration and automation. This is achieved by a combination of Clean Architecture principles, Microservices where applicable and Madallion architecture for near time reporting and ML use cases

Technologies

  • Openlink platform
  • .Net, Python, JVS & Open Components
  • Kafka as a streaming platform for system integration
  • Redis for caching
  • MS SQL Server
  • Databricks for reporting and ML use cases

Outcome & Impact

The case study is projected to result in a 20% increase in operational efficiency and a 15% reduction in costs for the client. The new system will improve data accuracy and provide real-time analytics, enabling better decision-making across the organization. Time to enter new markets and trade type supportability will increase by 50% and User satisfaction scores is expected to increase by 25%, reflecting the positive impact of the solution on daily operations, wider trade type coverage and improve time to market significantly.
